Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 June 2026

This is a starting template to make the page real — not legal advice. Have it reviewed before a public launch.

This policy explains what we collect, why, and the control you have over your data.

What we collect

  • Account details — your email and a securely hashed password (or your chosen sign-in provider), so you can log in.
  • Your conversations — stored so your chats save and sync across your devices.
  • Basic technical data — minimal logs needed to keep the service running and secure.

How we use it

To provide and improve Sevoria — signing you in, saving and syncing your chats, keeping the service safe, and making the assistant better over time. We do not sell your personal data.

Where it's stored & who can see it

Your account and conversations are kept with strict per-user access controls, so other users can't read your data. Replies are generated by our own AI service — we don't hand your conversations to advertisers.

Your choices

  • Export — request a copy of your data.
  • Delete — delete individual chats or your whole account at any time.
  • Correct — update your account details whenever you like.

Cookies

We use only essential cookies needed to keep you signed in. No advertising or tracking cookies.

Changes

If this policy changes, we'll update the date above and, for significant changes, let you know in the app.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact us.
