AI Transparency Notice

Effective date: June 26, 2026

Sevoria is an independent beta project. This page is written in plain English and in good faith; it is not a substitute for professional legal advice.

Sevoria's responses are generated by artificial intelligence. We want you to know exactly what that means.

What you're talking to

Sevoria is an AI language model — software that predicts helpful text. It is not a human, not a licensed professional, and has no real-world awareness. It does not "know" you and cannot take actions in the world on your behalf.

Outputs can be wrong

AI output may be inaccurate, incomplete, outdated, biased, or misleading, and may sound confident even when it is wrong. You are responsible for checking anything important before you rely on it. Sevoria is not a substitute for professional advice (legal, medical, financial, or otherwise).

When Sevoria uses the web

By default Sevoria answers from the model alone. If you turn on web search, it may retrieve and summarise public web results and cite their links; those third-party sources are not ours and we don't guarantee them.

Why we tell you this

Clear AI labelling is good practice and aligns with emerging transparency rules such as the EU AI Act. You'll also see an "AI-generated — verify important info" note under replies in the app.
