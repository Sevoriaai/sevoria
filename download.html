Put Sevoria on your phone

+300 credits when you install

Sevoria installs straight from your browser — no app store, no waiting. It gets its own icon and opens full-screen, just like a native app.

iPhone / iPad

  1. Open this site in Safari.
  2. Tap the Share button (the square with an up-arrow).
  3. Choose Add to Home Screen.
  4. Tap Add — Sevoria's on your home screen.

Then open it once to claim your +300 credits.

Android

  1. Open this site in Chrome.
  2. Tap Install below, or use the ⋮ menu → Install app.
  3. Confirm — Sevoria's on your home screen.

Then open it once to claim your +300 credits.
