About

An assistant with a spine.

Sevoria is a sharp, honest AI assistant — built for the things you actually do, not for a demo reel.

Why it exists

Most assistants flatter you, hedge everything, and sound exactly like each other. We wanted the opposite: one that gets to the point, pushes back when you're heading the wrong way, and says "I'm not sure" instead of confidently making things up.

What makes it different

  • Honest. It flags when it's unsure rather than bluffing.
  • Sharp. It answers the question you asked, without the padding.
  • Has a point of view. It'll tell you when an idea has a hole in it.

What we believe

  • Useful over impressive — being helpful beats sounding clever.
  • Honest over agreeable — you're better served by the truth.
  • Respect your time — get in, get the answer, get on with your day.
