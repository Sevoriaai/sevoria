Frequently asked questions

What is Sevoria?

A sharp, honest AI assistant for everyday things — writing, thinking something through, learning, and getting unstuck.

Is my data private?

Your chats are private to your account — other users can't see them, and we don't sell your data. We use conversations to operate and improve the service. The full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Which version am I using?

The free tier is Sevoria Core. A more capable Sevoria Pro is planned for people who want more power.

Is there a mobile app?

The web app works on your phone today, and a dedicated mobile app is on the way. Both share the same account and conversations, so everything stays in sync.

How do I delete my data?

From your account settings you can delete individual chats or your whole account at any time. See our Privacy Policy.

Is it free?

Yes — Sevoria Core is free to use.

How do I contact you?

Head to the contact page and send a note.
