Refund & Credits Policy

Effective date: June 26, 2026

Sevoria is an independent beta project. This page is written in plain English and in good faith; it is not a substitute for professional legal advice.

Paid plans (Pro and Max) are not live yet. This policy explains how refunds and credits will work once they are.

Refunds

  • Subscriptions can be cancelled anytime; you keep access until the end of the paid period. We don't pro-rate partial months unless the law requires it.
  • Credits are generally non-refundable once granted, except where required by law or in our reasonable discretion (e.g. a clear billing error).
  • Duplicate or accidental charges — contact us and we'll investigate and correct genuine errors.

Downtime

Sevoria is provided "as is" and may occasionally be unavailable. Short outages are not normally grounds for a refund, but if there's a prolonged failure we'll consider fair credit on request.

How to request

Email sevoriaai@gmail.com with your account email and (for billing issues) the transaction reference. See also Credit Usage.
